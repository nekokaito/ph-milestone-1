Gojo Senpai

Unmatched skills and an enigmatic personality.

Satoru Gojo is a fictional character from Gege Akutami's manga Jujutsu Kaisen. He was first introduced in Akutami's short series Tokyo Metropolitan Curse Technical School as the mentor of the cursed teenager Yuta Okkotsu at Tokyo Prefectural Jujutsu High School.

He is not just crazy strong, he is the strongest cursed technique user alive and the strongest Six Eyes technique user in the history of Jujutsu Kaisen. Moreover, Gojo inherited Limitless, the Gojo clan's legendary technique, which is one of the most powerful cursed methods in history.
